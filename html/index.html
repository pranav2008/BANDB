Welcome to Biryani and Breakfast

When you come to our restaurant, a waiter will sit you at an open table. When you finish deciding, you have to ring a bell to call for the waiter. This unique system allows our waitstaff to provide attentive service without hovering over your table. It also ensures that you can enjoy your meal without interruptions. Additionally, our waitstaff is trained to anticipate your needs and provide prompt service as soon as you ring the bell. This innovative approach enhances the overall dining experience for our customers. We believe that this method creates a more relaxed and enjoyable dining atmosphere for our guests. Our goal is to make sure you have a pleasant and seamless experience from start to finish.
